Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly does the platform setup include? ��
Our platform setup includes complete account registration, business verification, tax documentation (TRN setup), brand registry (if applicable), payment gateway integration, seller dashboard configuration, and initial policy compliance setup. We handle all paperwork and communication with the platform to get you approved and ready to sell.
How long does it take to go live on a platform? ▼
Typically, the setup process takes 7-14 business days depending on the platform and your business documentation readiness. Platforms like Noon and Amazon UAE require verification that can take 5-10 days. Fashion platforms like Namshi and Sivvi are usually faster at 3-7 days. We expedite wherever possible and keep you updated throughout.
Do I need a trade license to sell online? ▼
Yes, all major platforms in UAE require a valid trade license. You'll also need a Tax Registration Number (TRN) for most e-commerce platforms. We can guide you through obtaining these documents if you don't have them yet.
What's included in your product listing service? ▼
Each product listing includes: title optimization with keywords, detailed product descriptions, attribute mapping, category selection, pricing setup, inventory configuration, shipping details, and image upload (if you provide them). We ensure your listings are SEO-optimized to rank well in platform searches. For bulk uploads, we use templates to streamline the process.
Can you help with multiple platforms simultaneously? ▼
Absolutely! We specialize in multi-platform strategies. We can set you up on multiple e-commerce platforms (Amazon, Noon, Namshi, Carrefour, Dubizzle, etc.) simultaneously. This maximizes your reach and diversifies your revenue streams. We also help you manage inventory across platforms to avoid overselling.
What does order management service include? ▼
Our monthly order management includes: daily order monitoring and processing, inventory synchronization across platforms, customer inquiry handling, returns and refunds coordination, performance metrics reporting, pricing optimization recommendations, and promotional campaign setup. Think of us as your dedicated online operations team.
Why do I need professional product photography? ▼
Professional photos can increase conversion rates by 30-50%. Platforms favor high-quality images in search rankings, and customers trust products with clear, professional photos. Our photographers understand platform requirements (white backgrounds, multiple angles, size specifications) and create images that sell.
What are category approvals and why are they needed? ▼
Many categories on platforms like Amazon require pre-approval to ensure quality and authenticity. Restricted categories include beauty, health, baby products, electronics, and grocery. We handle the application process, provide necessary documentation (invoices, certificates, brand authorization), and follow up until approval. This typically takes 7-14 days per category.
Do you offer post-launch support? ▼
Yes! Beyond our ongoing order management service, we offer continued support including performance optimization, advertising campaign management, seasonal promotion planning, new product launches, policy compliance updates, and troubleshooting. We're invested in your long-term success, not just the initial setup.
Can I manage orders myself after training? ▼
Definitely! Our comprehensive training program teaches you or your team everything needed to independently manage your online presence. We cover order processing, inventory management, customer service, performance analysis, and marketing basics. Many clients start with our management service and transition to self-management after gaining confidence.
What's the difference between selling on marketplaces vs. having my own website? ▼
Marketplaces (Amazon, Noon, Namshi) give you immediate access to millions of customers but charge commission (15-25%). Your own website gives you full control, customer data, and no commissions, but requires marketing to drive traffic. We recommend a hybrid approach: start on marketplaces for quick sales, then build your website for long-term brand building and better margins.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept bank transfers, cheques, and can invoice your company. For our complete package, we offer installment options: 50% upfront and 50% upon going live. Monthly services are billed in advance. All prices are in AED and exclude VAT (5% will be added to invoices).
