Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What exactly does the platform setup include? ▼
Our platform setup includes complete account registration, business verification, tax documentation (TRN setup), brand registry (if applicable), payment gateway integration, seller dashboard configuration, and initial policy compliance setup. We handle all paperwork and communication with the platform to get you approved and ready to sell.
How long does it take to go live on a platform? ▼
Typically, the setup process takes 7-14 business days depending on the platform and your business documentation readiness. Platforms like Noon and Amazon UAE require verification that can take 5-10 days. We expedite wherever possible and keep you updated throughout.
Do I need a trade license to sell online? ▼
Yes, all major platforms in UAE require a valid trade license. You'll also need a Tax Registration Number (TRN) for most e-commerce platforms. We can guide you through obtaining these documents if you don't have them yet.
What's included in your product listing service? ▼
Each product listing includes: title optimization with keywords, detailed product descriptions, attribute mapping, category selection, pricing setup, inventory configuration, shipping details, and image upload (if you provide them). We ensure your listings are SEO-optimized to rank well in platform searches.
Can you help with multiple platforms simultaneously? ▼
Absolutely! We specialize in multi-platform strategies. We can set you up on multiple e-commerce platforms (Amazon, Noon, Namshi, Carrefour, Dubizzle, etc.) simultaneously. This maximizes your reach and diversifies your revenue streams.
What does order management service include? ▼
Our monthly order management includes: daily order monitoring and processing, inventory synchronization across platforms, customer inquiry handling, returns and refunds coordination, performance metrics reporting, pricing optimization recommendations, and promotional campaign setup.
